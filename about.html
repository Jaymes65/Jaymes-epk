About Me – Jaymes65

I’m Jaymes T Manyange, professionally known as Jaymes65—a DJ, creative, and storyteller through sound. From the first time I stood behind the decks, I knew I wasn’t just here to play music—I was here to craft moments.

Over the past three years, I’ve brought electrifying energy to stages across clubs, festivals, lounges, and private events. Every performance is more than just a playlist—it’s an experience, a vibe, a connection.

My Style

At the heart of my sound is Afro House, but I blend it with Deep House, Afro Tech, Amapiano, and even Hip Hop. Each set is unique—tailored to the moment and the energy of the crowd.

Where I Started

My journey began in 2022 in Centurion, Gauteng, playing small local gigs. With time, passion, and dedication I’ve grown to perform at respected venues like Cubana Fourways, News Café Mall@55, African Chef, News Café Cresta, R55 ChillOut and The Palms Backyard.
